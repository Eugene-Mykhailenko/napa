Napa and Sonoma, one of the most romantic and beautiful places on planet earth. A paradise where one can find harmony with nature and appreciate amazing architectures of past and modern design of present. Furthermore Napa/Sonoma is a home of over 800 wineries, many of which are considered the best of its kind; a lot of people travel to Napa and Sonoma to enjoy sophisticated hotels, spas and amazing Michelin star restaurants located in this area.

So, how do people get to Napa and Sonoma? There are certainly many options from any hotel, residence or Airport; one can take a bus, shuttle, rent a car or, in our opinion the most comfortable and effective choice is to take a car service, where folks can combine transportation to a destination in Napa as well as possibility to take Napa Wine Tour before getting to the final destination. If you are just taking a tour through Napa Wineries and not staying in Napa, then a professional Car Service/Limo company is the only option that will allow you to see and enjoy the most.
